Missionary Dinner Calendar

Please sign up to provide dinner for the missionaries serving in the Lake Oswego Ward. You can sign up for one or both companionships on the same evening. Include your email address to receive a reminder email two days before the dinner appointment. If a dinner slot has a "SACK" watermark, the meal should be a sack dinner delivered to the Portland Temple Visitors' Center.

Recommended times: 6pm for dinner in your home, 3-6pm for sack dinners. Times are flexible. Questions? Send email to LOmissionarymeals@gmail.com.
